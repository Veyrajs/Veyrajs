The camera owns the view transform. World coordinates stay fixed; the camera zooms and pans over
them and converts between **screen** and **world** space (`screenToWorld` / `worldToScreen`).

## Zoom & pan

Drag to pan; scroll to zoom about the cursor. The readout shows the live zoom and the world
coordinate under the pointer:
